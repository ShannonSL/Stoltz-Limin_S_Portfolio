Welcome to Me

My name is Shannon Stoltz-Limin, I'm a 2nd year student at Fanshawe College, taking the Interactive Media and Production program. I'm a writer, highly creative, and an avid videogamer. There's always a pen and notebook with me incase of a sudden burst of inspiration, and I never leave home without a book. Every year during November; I try to take part in a month long writing "competition" called NaNoWriMo (National Novel Writing Month), it's a lot of fun and a great way to meet people at the local write-ins. One day I'm hoping that a publisher will pick up one of my manuscripts and offer me a contract. I've also found that I have a love for making simple animations and pretty things in Cinema 4D. After following a tutorial and modifiying it so I was happy, I designed the Cover image for this very website!
